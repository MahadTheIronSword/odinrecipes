Fried Chicken

Mozzarella sticks are elongated pieces of battered or breaded mozzarella.

Ingredients

  • 2 beaten eggs
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cups of Italian seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic salt
  • 2/3 cup of all purpose flour
  • 1/3 cup of cornstarch
  • 1 quart of oil
  • 1 16 ounce package of mozzerella cheese sticks

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix the eggs and water.
  2. Mix the bread crumbs and garlic salt in a medium bowl. In a separate medium bowl, blend the flour and cornstarch.
  3. In a large heavy saucepan, heat the oil to 365 degrees F (185 degrees C).
  4. One at a time, coat each mozzarella stick in the flour mixture, then the egg mixture, then in the bread crumbs and finally into the oil. Fry until golden brown, about 30 seconds. Remove from heat and drain on paper towels.
